Typed coreR.

We are seeking for a system that would provide both static informations and a safe way to gradually define a program that could contain free variables. We consider free variables as effects whose role is to statically analyse when the user defines a computation that would be stuck because of a free variable. We consider simple assignements, super assignements and free variables as effects. Effects can be propagated, composed etc..
Effects allow us to distinguish between:
- pure programs: those that do not contain free variables.
- semi-pure programs: those that contain free variables but their evaluation is not stuck.
- stuck programs: those that contain free variables and their evaluation is stuck.

Examples: Simple Super0 Super1 Propagation1 Semi0
